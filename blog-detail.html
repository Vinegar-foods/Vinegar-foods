Sublimation place for family parties

VINEGAR + Jun 02, 2022

Having given it some thought, I’m pretty sure BBQ might quite possibly be the best hobby there is. Or at least for me it is.

BBQ refers to long slow cooks at low temperatures. This is primarily used for large cuts of meat with a high fat concentration. The long slow cooking process breaks down the fat and tenderizes the meat in the most magical way.

What other hobby allows you to sit back with your favorite beverage next to your friends on a warm spring or summer day, with your cooking hat cocked just right, eyes open just enough to know what the dogs are up to, and still be able to claim “Not now I’m busy”?

Like a lot of guys my age, comes a time when you realize in truth you’d just rather spend time with your family than anybody else. Therefore, BBQ party is a perfect hobby.
