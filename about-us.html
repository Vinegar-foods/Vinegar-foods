About us

See you at Vinegar Food's restaurant

Since 1965, Vinegar Food Restaurants have won countless culinary awards and become an important part of the communities they serve.

We are passionate about providing the freshest seafood flown in daily, prime steaks, our extensive wine list recognized by Wine Spectator, and knowledgeable, attentive service.

NGUYEN DANH HUNG

FOUNDER, RAY'S RESTAURANTS

Founded in 1965 by renowned restaurant industry veteran NGUYEN DANH HUNG, Vinegar Restaurants, LLC, headquartered in Marietta, includes a chain of over 1000 restaurants worldwide. Each restaurant features an award-winning menu with a focus on fresh fish caught daily from the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, prime cuts of ribs, fine steaks, and extensive wines from around the world.

"We want every guest to have an exceptional dining experience in a refined, yet warm and inviting setting," says HUNG.

We look forward to you dining with us soon!

LE TUAN MINH

PRESIDENT & CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Minh motivation stems from her desire to combine analytical skills with training and development. Having worked for Hillstone Restaurant Group for over 15 years in several positions truly helped his cultivate and shape many aspects that are important to every successful business, while building a foundation of excellence. Minh worked in many locations while with Hillstone, from NYC to Miami to Atlanta, allows his to understand the many different needs of various demographics and infuse new techniques.

"My passion for the training and development side of operations started early in my career, as I was captivated by the implementation of systems and soon wanted to learn every detail and nuance" says MINH. From there he grew a profound desire to share this knowledge and grow his team similarly. This drive is exactly why he joined the Vinegar family in 1990 as corporate trainer. His focus at Vinegar is to embrace the family feeling and build cohesion throughout all three stores. MINH daily goals are to initiate and implement standards for delivering state of the art service, and continually grow and develop each individual, all while adapting to the evolving trends of food and operations. MINH dedication to the business and his people are directly related to creating that perfect experience for our guests.

BUI VAN DUNG

CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

DUNG is an Atlanta native who received his accounting degree from the University of Georgia. He began his career as auditor with EY. Later in his career he has held CFO roles in industries that include aviation, computer hardware, medical software, consumer packaged goods and infant toys. He was worked with iconic global brands including Eastman Kodak and Kids II.

DUNG has followed Vinegar and his many restaurant brands since his early childhood and is proud to be a part of Vinegar. As a former small business owner, DUNG enjoys the entrepreneurial spirit of Vinegar restaurants and is excited to share his financial experience with the Vinegar team.

DINH CONG BANG

DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONS

BANG grew up in Chicago and is a hospitality industry veteran with more than 20 years of restaurant management experience. BANGstudied at Northern Illinois University and has managed such notable restaurants as Bella Vista, Bacino's of Naperville and Wildfire in Illinois. He was relocated to Atlanta with Wildfire, where he served as General Manager for four years. He joined the Vinegar family in 2011 as the Operating Partner of Vinegar and in 2015 became the Operating Partner for Vinegar at NewYork in addition to the River location. He was recently promoted to Director of Operations for Vinegar Food.

BANG enjoys the energy and fast pace of the restaurant industry. His focus at Vinegar is to capitalize on the reputation the restaurant has built, while always looking for ways to continuously evolve the food, service and overall dining experience while adapting to the trending ways of the industry.

While his title remains as "Director of Operations", he defines his role as "Director of People." He is incredibly passionate about his people and believes that creating a positive work environment is directly related to a positive dining experience for our guests.
